Physis: Portuguese Association of Physics Students

Special Contributors:

Joana Leiria, Mario Cristovão, Rita Barradas, Joana Moura, Silvia Santos, Ariana, Constança Alves, Afonso Avila, Inês Serra.
2019/22

I joined Physis to help create social media content to promote the ENEF event, a national gathering for Physics students in Portugal. As I showcased my skills in graphic design, video editing and typography, I became a permanent member of Physis and soon took on the role of coordinating the image and communication team, implementing a Kanban system using Excel and Trello to streamline team management, reduce misunderstandings, and speed up design development. I led the creation and development of designs for various mediums like publications, websites, flyers, posters, magazines and videos. During this time, I also served as President of the Fiscal Council. Thanks to my dedication, I became treasurer in the following term, gaining a position on the executive board. This role allowed me to apply and refine my understanding of accountability learned during my degree.

Project: Mochu

Other Contributors:

João Caravalho, Rafael Amarel
2017/18

The Mochu project was developed during my highschool year and the aim was to stop wildfires by using small devices in forests. When one device burned, it sent messages with its location. Lots of these devices together could map out how fires started and spread. We built a prototype and entered it into national contests. We got three honorable mentions and won the top prize of Novas Fronteiras da Engenharia contest, in 2018.

2014/15

This documentary is perhaps one of my first major projects. It centers around my 9th-grade class, featuring my friends and a few teachers. I began this project because of my fascination with design, movies, and typography. However, what truly drove me was the joy I felt being part of such a close-knit class - we were great friends and I wanted to preserve our moments. For this project, I delved into graphic design, taught myself manual photography techniques, explored video creation like image composition, camera position and sound engineering.
