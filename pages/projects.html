Physis: Portuguese Association of Physics Students

Special Contributors: Joana Leiria, Mario Cristovão , Rita Barradas, Joana Moura, Silvia Santos, , Ariana, , Constança Alves, , Afonso Avila , Inês Serra.

Year: 2019/21

I entered Physis to design publications to social networks to promote de ENEF event. This event gathers students of physics around Portugal. This event requires a lot of teamwork, management e quality given its dimension. As I proved my skills in graphic design, video editing and typography skills, I asked to be a permenant menber of Physis. I became the image and comunication tema coordenator. I developed an kanban system with excel and trello to manage my team and to reduce misinterpretations and time developing design. I made, helped and directed the develop of design for publications, websites, flyers, posters and videos. During this time, I also was president of the fiscal council. By showing rigour in my work, I became treasurer in the next mandate and, as such, a member of the running board. In this role I applied and improved what I learned about accountability in my degree.

Project: Mochu

Other Contributors: João Caravalho, Rafael Amarel

Year:2017/18

This project was aimed to prevent wildfires by making small devices that would be placed in forests. When the deviced burned, it would send messages with it's position. A lot of this devices togheter would map out the begining and propagation of wild fires. We made a prototype and went to various national contests. We receibed various honarable mentions. Fortanutly, we won the prize Novas Fronteiras da Engenharia in 2018.
