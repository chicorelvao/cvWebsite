Physis: Portuguese Association of Physics Students

Special Contributors: Joana Leiria, Mario Cristovão, Rita Barradas, Joana Moura, Silvia Santos, Ariana, Constança Alves, Afonso Avila, Inês Serra.

Year: 2019/21

I joined Physis to design publications for social media to promote the ENEF event, a national event that gathers Physic students from Portugal. Given its importance, this event requires a lot of teamwork, management and quality content. As I proved my skills in graphic design, video editing and typography skills, I asked to be a permenant menber of Physis. I became the image and comunication team coordenator, where I developed an kanban system with excel and trello to manage my team and to reduce misinterpretations and the time in development of designs. I created, directed and helped the development of design for publications, websites, flyers, posters and videos. During this time, I also became President of the Fiscal Vouncil. By showing diligence in my work, in the next mandate, I became treasurer and, consequently, a member of the running board. In this role, I applied and improved what I learned about accountability in my degree.

Project: Mochu

Other Contributors: João Caravalho, Rafael Amarel

Year:2017/18

Project: Mochu was aimed to prevent wildfires by developing small devices to be placed in forests. When one deviced burned, it would send messages with it's position and, as such, a lot of this devices togheter would map out the begining and propagation of wild fires. We made a prototype and submitted it to various national contests. With that, we received three honorable mentions and, fortunately, won the first place and prize of Novas Fronteiras da Engenharia in 2018.

Rosas Picantes

Other Contributors: Beatriz Maduro, Sofia Coelho

Year:2019/21

This documentary is perhaps my first big project. It is about my 9th grade class, friends and some teachers. I started it due to my interest in design, movies and typography, but the most important factor was the feeling of really enjoying my class – we were really good friends and that was such a happy memory that I wanted to capture and maintain. For this project, during my free time, I started reading about graphic design, learning how to shoot manual photography, making videos and 3D animations and experimenting with photoshop to its full potential.
