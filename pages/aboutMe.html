Scouts Moviment

1086 Palheira - 2006/2015

603 Antanhol - 2020/2021

I joined the scouts movement when I was 6 years old. When I was a "lobito" - young wolf - I developed a sense of orienting my actions and decisions by moral values typical of heroes movies. Later on, it was very important to plane head, in group, the camps: where to sleep and visit, some activities and games to pass time. We even organized events to raise money to fund material and the activities for the group. It was also crucial having to develop some soft skills like: developing friendshid, communication, stewardship, accounting, stress tenacity, group arguments, pioneering construtions, physical ability, orientation in unknow places with maps and compass. In regional, national and international activities it was essencial to be an open and fun person so that meetings strangers could be done quickly in order to attain certain objectives. One of the most rewarding and significant parts of my personal path on scouts was serving and helping the local community where we were established.
